NotEye includes the DawnLike tileset (see the "dawnlike" subdirectory).
It was created by DragonDePlatino under the CC BY-SA 3.0 license
http://opengameart.org/content/dawnlike-16x16-universal-rogue-like-tileset-v10
It is used by the samples.

Examples were removed from the original package, and fonts (SDS6x6 and SDS8x8) were
converted from TTF tothe NotEye PNG format.

"RLTiles". You can find the original tileset at: http://rltiles.sf.net

(These tiles are in the file rltiles.png, and they are used by nLarn and Rogue)
